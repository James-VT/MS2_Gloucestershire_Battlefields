Gloucestershire Battlefields Society

Caring for battlefields and historical sites across Gloucestershire and South Gloucestershire since 2002

Welcome to the website of the Gloucestershire Battlefields Society. We created this website to share our knowledge and raise awareness of the work we do. We are a small group of friends and volunteers who have been caring for historic sites in Gloucestershire and South Gloucestershire for almost two decades. Click here to learn more.

Here, you can learn more about the sites we care for and their history. We perform essential groundworks and litterpicks on the sites we look after, raise funds for their upkeep, and can facilitate tours of the battlefields for pre-booked groups of visitors. For more information on arranging a tour to visit one of our sites, you can click our "contact form" link in the header. To learn about the sites we look after, you can use our interactive map by clicking the "map" link. Or if you want to put your knowldge of local military history to the test, click "quiz" and take our interactive challenge.

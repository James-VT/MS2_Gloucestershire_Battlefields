Gloucestershire Battlefields Society

Caring for battlefields and historical sites across Gloucestershire and South Gloucestershire since 2002

Welcome to our interactive map! Here you can learn about the sites we care for and the events that took place on them hundreds or even thousands of years ago. Either click the markers on the map itself or the buttons below to learn more. The information will appear underneath the buttons. Then, if you think you've learned all there is to know, head over to our quiz page and put your knowledge to the test!
